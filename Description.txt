**Slingboom** is a turn-based shooter game between two aliens on your phone. You control an alien character using a slingshot to shoot down opponents in a space full of obstacles!

## ğŸ® BASIC GAMEPLAY STEPS

### **Step 1: Select a Card**
- Each turn you will have **4 cards** in your hand.
- Each card costs a different amount of **Energy**.
- Tap on the card to select the type of bullet you want to shoot.

### **Step 2: Drag to Aim**
- After selecting a card, **drag your finger** on the screen to aim.
- Drag **down** = shoot upwards.
- Drag **left** = shoot to the right.
- Drag **right** = shoot to the left.
- The colored line shows the **bullet trajectory**.

### **Step 3: Release to Shoot**
- **Release your finger** to fire the bullet.

- The bullet will fly along the aimed trajectory.

- Try to hit the enemy or destroy the obstacle.

### **Step 4: End Your Turn**
- After Shoot all cards or run out of energy, press the **"END TURN"** button.

- Or wait for the time to run out (30 seconds) and the turn will automatically end.

## âš¡ ENERGY SYSTEM
